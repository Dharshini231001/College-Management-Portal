KSA was established by Kongu Vellalar Institute of Technology Trust in the year 2015 with the approval of Council of Architecture, New Delhi and affiliated to Anna University, Chennai to offer B.Arch course with an intake of 40 students from the academic year 2015-16 with a vision to provide value-based education to nurture and inspire students for design practice and explore knowledge to meet global requirements.
